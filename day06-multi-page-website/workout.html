Weekly Workout Plan

  • Monday: Chest Day
  • Tuesday: Back Day
  • Wednesday: Shoulder Day
  • Thursday: Biceps Day
  • Friday: Leg Day
  • Saturday: Triceps Cardio Abs
  • Monday – Chest Day
    • Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets × 6–10 reps
    • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets × 8–12 reps
    • Chest Fly (Machine or Dumbbell) – 3 sets × 10–15 reps
    • Push-ups – 3 sets × max reps
    • Cable Crossover – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Tuesday – Back Day
    • Pull-ups (or Lat Pulldown) – 4 sets × 6–10 reps
    • Barbell Rows – 3 sets × 8–12 reps
    • Seated Cable Row – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Deadlifts – 3 sets × 5–8 reps
    • Face Pulls – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Wednesday – Shoulder Day
    • Overhead Dumbbell Press – 4 sets × 6–10 reps
    • Lateral Raises – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
    • Front Raises – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Rear Delt Fly – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
    • Shrugs – 3 sets × 10–15 reps
  • Thursday – Biceps Day
    • Barbell Curl – 4 sets × 8–12 reps
    • Dumbbell Alternating Curl – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Hammer Curl – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Preacher Curl – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Concentration Curl – 2–3 sets × 12 reps
  • Friday – Leg Day
    • Squats – 4 sets × 6–10 reps
    • Leg Press – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Lunges – 3 sets × 10 reps per leg
    • Leg Curl (Hamstrings) – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
    • Calf Raises – 4 sets × 12–20 reps
  • Saturday – Triceps + Cardio/Abs
    • Triceps:
    • Close-Grip Bench Press – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
    • Tricep Pushdown – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Overhead Tricep Extension – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
    • Dips – 3 sets × max reps
    • Abs:
    • Crunches – 3 sets × 15–20 reps
    • Leg Raises – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
    • Plank – 3 sets × 30–60 sec
    • Cardio:
    • Running / Cycling – 20–30 minutes (moderate intensity)
